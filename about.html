About Our Plastic Recycling Facility

We are a group of passionate students who care about the environment and want to protect it for posterity. However at the same, we can't stop the science for the betterment of tomorrow. Sustainable research and development is the goal we want to work towards in order to save today and tomorrow in one generation at a time. As the future of tomorrow, it is our responsibility to think about the betterment of the future but at the same time, contribute to the research of our time and save the world.
